Bodhi Fitness

What We Do

My fundamental belief is that health and fitness should be enjoyable and not complicated! I try to emphasise this in everything I do. Whether it is through diet advice, programme design or mindset techniques. The word ‘Bodhi’ traditionally translates into English as enlightenment, but the literal meaning is ‘awakening’. For me this is an important phrase, everyone has a story or reason they want or need to exercise, but sometimes we need that extra guidance and advice to take that first step to awaken our fitness journey. Whether it be through exercise advice, or giving encouragement to get you through the session! We are in this together, there will be hard work ahead but ultimately we will have fun with it and enjoy the journey to a healthier you.
